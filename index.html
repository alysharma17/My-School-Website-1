Attendance Rules


It is the parent’s/guardian’s responsibility to ensure that students attend school promptly and regularly. There are some times when the student is allowed to leave school early or not attend. Some examples are health issues, doctor's appointments,observance of a religious holiday, court appearance, funeral of immediate family member, and classes.
